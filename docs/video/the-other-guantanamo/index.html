The Other Guantanamo

Short Documentary Film

Launch Date: October, 2023

This short video brings viewers to the bi-annual Changüí Festival in Guantánamo, Cuba to explore the music and culture of changüí, an Afro-Cuban “roots” music particular to the region that originated in the early 19th-century sugar cane refineries and rural mountain slave communities.
